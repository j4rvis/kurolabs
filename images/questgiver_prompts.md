## Prompt Structure Reference

```
NPC, [adjective] [age] [gender] [race] [role], questgiver for [category], focus on character, [background scene description], [props/items in hand or nearby], [expression or posture], square
```

**Tips:**
- Keep background detailed but secondary to the character
- Include 2–3 specific props that signal the category
- Specify age and expression to match the quest giver's personality
- "focus on character" and "square" are load-bearing terms for the right crop and framing
